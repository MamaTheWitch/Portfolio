Treasure hunt app for kids birthday

Level UP

Magic happens when your teenager is too old for clowns, but too young for bars, and you have to come up with a birthday party plan for him. When you want kids to have active games outdoors, and they prefer Fortnite'ing indoors... mama creates an app with hints about locations where the code is hidden. Once the right code is entered, you get another location, jump on your bike and go there. This way you keep exploring the area until reaching the finish/party location.
GitHub repository Recipe: #React.js
